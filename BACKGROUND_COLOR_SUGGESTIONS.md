# Background Color Suggestions for House of Healing Logo

Based on your logo with light blue, gold/yellow, and white elements, here are color suggestions:

## Recommended Options

### Option 1: Deep Navy Blue (Recommended)
- **Hero**: `#1a2332` or `#0f1419` (dark navy)
- **Why**: Complements the light blue in your logo, creates elegant contrast
- **Sidebar**: `#1a2332` (matches hero)
- **CSS**: `background: #1a2332;` or `background: linear-gradient(135deg, #1a2332 0%, #0f1419 100%);`

### Option 2: Warm Dark Brown/Burgundy
- **Hero**: `#2d1b1e` or `#3d2a2d` (warm dark brown)
- **Why**: Complements the gold/yellow flames, creates warmth
- **Sidebar**: `#2d1b1e`
- **CSS**: `background: linear-gradient(135deg, #2d1b1e 0%, #1a1214 100%);`

### Option 3: Rich Teal/Deep Cyan
- **Hero**: `#0a2e36` or `#1a4a52` (deep teal)
- **Why**: Harmonizes with the light blue in logo, spiritual feel
- **Sidebar**: `#0a2e36`
- **CSS**: `background: linear-gradient(135deg, #0a2e36 0%, #051a1f 100%);`

### Option 4: Deep Purple-Blue (Current, but refined)
- **Hero**: `#2d1b4e` or `#1a0f3d` (deeper, richer purple)
- **Why**: Keeps current theme but more sophisticated
- **Sidebar**: `#2d1b4e`
- **CSS**: `background: linear-gradient(135deg, #2d1b4e 0%, #1a0f3d 100%);`

### Option 5: Charcoal Gray with Blue Tint
- **Hero**: `#1e2832` or `#2a3441` (charcoal with blue undertone)
- **Why**: Neutral, professional, lets logo stand out
- **Sidebar**: `#1e2832`
- **CSS**: `background: linear-gradient(135deg, #1e2832 0%, #141a20 100%);`

## Color Psychology for Bible Studies

- **Navy Blue**: Trust, stability, depth, wisdom
- **Warm Brown**: Grounded, earthy, traditional
- **Teal/Cyan**: Peace, spirituality, healing
- **Deep Purple**: Wisdom, spirituality, reverence
- **Charcoal**: Professional, timeless, readable
